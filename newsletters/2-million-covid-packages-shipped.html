COVID-19 Vaccine

February 4, 2021
Experts Liam Donaldson (D.P.H., Ed.D), Carter Miller (D.P.A., Ed.D)

Vaccines are starting to be distributed in America. The older people get them may depend on your state, but here are some of the general guidelines distribution might follow. Initial vaccines will be given to healthcare workers.

Other essential workers and high-risk groups, such as the elderly, will come next. As vaccine supplies become more abundant, they will be distributed to other groups next. These regulations help minimize death and disease. The overall goal of the vaccine is to train people's immune system to remember COVID-19's material and to fight it when it comes in contact with the individual. Scientists hope this will decrease the spread of the virus and make people who have come into contact with it less contagious.
